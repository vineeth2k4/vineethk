Figuring out – Discovery

As a student, I found myself exploring a path in accountancy during my secondary grades, but deep down, I knew something was missing. While numbers and finances intrigued me, my heart always gravitated towards creativity. I felt a pull towards design and art—something that allowed me to express myself and make an impact.

I wanted to pursue something meaningful, something worth dedicating my life to. It wasn't just about finding a career; it was about choosing a path that aligned with my desire for purpose and creativity.



Stories Through the Lens

My journey into creativity didn’t stop with design and art—I also discovered a passion for photography. Capturing moments through the lens allows me to see the world from new perspectives and preserve stories in a single frame. Photography gives me a sense of freedom and connection, letting me explore and express myself in ways beyond words. It's become a way for me to blend my love for art with the beauty of everyday life.

Exploration Awaits...

Travel has woven itself into the fabric of my life, each journey like a chapter filled with vibrant cultures, stunning landscapes, and untold stories. Every new destination pulls me out of my comfort zone, inviting me to experience the world through different eyes. With every adventure, I find inspiration for my creative pursuits, collecting moments and memories that shape not only my art but also my view of life. To me, travel is more than just movement—it’s a journey of discovery, a chance to turn the ordinary into the extraordinary.
